Evolution of NLP

Major milestones in Natural Language Processing development.

1950s

Early computational linguistics and machine translation research begin.

1990s

Statistical language models and probabilistic approaches become dominant.

2013

Word2Vec introduces efficient neural word embeddings and semantic representation.

2017

Transformer architecture revolutionizes sequence modeling and attention mechanisms.

Today

Large Language Models, multimodal AI, and generative systems power intelligent applications worldwide.
