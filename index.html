About This Repository

This website is developed as a Knowledge Artifact Repository for Week 1 of the Natural Language Processing course. It provides structured learning materials including concept cards, comparison tables, workflow diagrams, industrial applications, research insights and sustainability impact of NLP. The repository is designed to help beginners understand fundamental NLP concepts through examples, illustrations and practical applications.
