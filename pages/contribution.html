📌 Project Overview

The NLP Knowledge Artifact Repository is an academic web-based knowledge platform developed to provide structured information about Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, Language Models, Text Processing Techniques, Research Trends, Applications, and Sustainability.

The repository serves as an educational resource for students, researchers, and technology enthusiasts interested in learning about modern NLP concepts through a professional and interactive website.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Provide comprehensive NLP learning resources.
  • Explain core NLP concepts in a structured format.
  • Demonstrate NLP workflows visually.
  • Compare major NLP techniques and models.
  • Showcase real-world NLP applications.
  • Explore current NLP research trends.
  • Highlight sustainability and social impact.
  • Create a professional academic repository.
