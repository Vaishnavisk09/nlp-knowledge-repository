NLP Pipeline Overview

The NLP processing pipeline converts unstructured text into meaningful information through multiple preprocessing and feature engineering stages before applying machine learning algorithms for prediction and analysis.

TF-IDF Importance

TF-IDF is one of the most widely used feature extraction techniques that assigns importance to words based on frequency within a document and rarity across documents.

Transformer Architecture

Transformers utilize self-attention mechanisms to process language efficiently, enabling advanced models such as BERT, GPT, and modern Large Language Models.
